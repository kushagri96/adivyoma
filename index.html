About Us

Team Adi Vyoma is the official student rocketry team of the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES).

The team represents UPES at the International Rocket Engineering Competition (IREC), one of the world's largest intercollegiate rocketry competitions held in the United States.

Our mission is to design, build and launch high-performance sounding rockets while pushing the boundaries of student-led aerospace engineering.

Through interdisciplinary collaboration across avionics, structures, propulsion, recovery and payload systems, the team aims to deliver reliable flight systems and advance hands-on aerospace learning.
